The Surgeon

Dr. Rushil Jain is a premier Laparoscopic, Bariatric, Robotic, and General Surgeon working at the leading edge of minimally invasive medical strategies. Having developed clinical foundations across India's hallmark medical centers including AIIMS, New Delhi, and Maulana Azad Medical College, his methodologies consistently highlight patient-first surgical diagnostics.

Currently guiding clinical spaces as a key consultant at Max Super Speciality Hospital, Saket, Dr. Jain synthesizes high-tier academic research with customized, state-of-the-art technological systems to assure optimized recovery journeys for compound abdominal wall and bariatric operations.
