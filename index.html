The Surgeon

Dr. Rushil Jain is a highly accomplished surgeon specializing in advanced Minimally Invasive, Bariatric, and Robotic surgical pathways. Armed with memberships and fellowships from elite institutions internationally, including the Royal College of Surgeons (England), his professional roadmap reflects an unwavering commitment to cutting-edge clinical execution and patient-centric recovery metrics.

From foundational residencies at institutions like AIIMS and Maulana Azad Medical College, to modern leadership as a key consultant at Max Super Speciality Hospital Saket, his clinical scope ensures world-class surgical care utilizing state-of-the-art diagnostic and operative technologies.
